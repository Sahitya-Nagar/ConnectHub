Frequently asked questions

For step-by-step instructions for setting up an eSIM on an Android or an Apple phone and to get answers to more frequently asked questions about the process, check out our Help page.
Any unlocked phone works with a Koodo SIM. Phones sold in Canada after December 1, 2017, are already unlocked.

To check:

Insert a Koodo SIMif prompted for an unlock code or "SIM Not Supported," it's locked.

For iPhones, go to Settings > General > About > Carrier Lock.

If locked, please contact your carrier to unlock it.
An eSIM is an industry-standard digital SIM that allows you to activate a cellular plan from Koodo without having to use a SIM card. Most newer phone models already come with eSIM support. Learn more
